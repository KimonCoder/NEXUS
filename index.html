NEXUS 2.0

A nexus of ideas, motivation and a lot more.

NEXUS is an annual tech-based event held at Apeejay School, Pitampura. It's more important to get experience in a competitive environment than it is to win or lose. So thrive and join the NEXUS. What you get-:

  • Experience
  • Certificates of Participation
  • Exciting Prizes to all winners
